I am a M.S. student in Computer Science and Technology at the School of Software Engineering, Xi'an Jiaotong University (XJTU), advised by Prof. Yihong Gong. I received my B.S. degree in Mathematics from the School of Mathematics and Statistics, XJTU in 2024. I am currently a Research Intern at ByteDance Seed, working on VLM pretraining and unified VLA systems. Previously, I was a Research Intern at Alibaba DAMO Academy, Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA), and ByteDance Seed.

Most of my previous research focuses on vision foundation models, spanning model design, large-scale pretraining, dataset collection, and benchmark evaluation. Currently, I am dedicated to advancing the intelligence of unified multimodal models — pushing them to perceive, reason, and act more like genuinely intelligent systems.
